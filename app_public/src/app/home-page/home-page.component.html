About Us

When in Waterloo, don't miss a trip to Baking Bella. Residents consider The Bakery, with its rustic outdoor cafe and beautiful gardens, to be the center of social life in Waterloo, a place to meet people, bump into old friends, or sit quietly and read the papers. Known since 1981 for great bagels, croissants, rolls, rugulah, danish and butter cookies, Baking Bella includes a coffee bar and full lunch menu. Experience Waterloo! Visit Baking Bella.
